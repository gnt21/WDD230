Description of the Target Audience

The objective is to identify entities with similar needs and connect them with entities that provide the service or product they need. So the audience is companies and freelancers (of all types and sizes).

Personas

Persona: Hospitais

Their need is to find companies or freelancers that print parts of oxygen breathing equipment on a 3D printer.

Persona: Small 3D printing company

Its goal is to grow by providing custom 3D prints.

Scenarios

1. I'm a small company, can I join other companies to get volume to buy cheaper?

2. I have a process that is not my company's core. Can I find companies interested in absorbing this process?

3. I need a raw material but I don't know where to find it, is there a centralized catalog on raw materials supplied by companies in the region?

4. I am interested in opening a business, where can I see the demand that exists today for the type of service I want to provide?

5. I need to buy a product and I would like to search for the best price. Is there a channel where I can do an auction?
